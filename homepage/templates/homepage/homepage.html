ABOUT US

PURPOSE

Simply, our purpose is to begin the conversation, it’s to make perpetrators think twice before they make the wrong decision, it’s to educate about what has been kept silent and to reach out to those who need someone. We are here for you - we are an active page that involves a small number of people who are devoted to help those who reach out. We are listening to you - we are smaller than a hand full of people who are great listeners and are always ready to be all ears when needed. We are active - we will not stay idle when it comes to issues you have raised. We will address them in our best possible way and push you towards resolving it through the best means. WE ARE HERE, WE ARE LISTENING AND WE ARE ACTIVE.

VISION

STRONGER TOGETHER STRONGER TOGETHER is more than just an organisation - it is a safe space for EVERYONE to talk about their issues, what they believe strongly about and raise awareness while giving power to the voiceless. We are OPEN to EVERYONE - LGBTQ2S+ (Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer or Questioning and Two-Spirit) Women, Men and other. With frequent events involving: informative sessions, educational workshops, the use of creative arts therapy (dance, art, music, writing, meditation), retreats, campaigns we aim to focus on the issues revolving around Mental Health and wellbeing including depression, anxiety, stress, sexual harassment (consent, bad touch, sexual health, prevention) and anything and everything that alters our physical, emotional, psychological and social well-being. WE ARE HERE, WE ARE LISTENING AND WE ARE ACTIVE. If you have anything you wish to discuss about please do not hesitate to contact us. Once we have our anonymous portal ready, we hope more of you can share your experiences, awareness and anything you have to say with us so more people are not victims to what you may be experiencing or have been experiencing. **Please be advised that we take the Australian Laws very seriously and align our activities in consideration to the privacy of both parties (victim and abusers) to full effect. THIS IS NOT A NAME AND SHAME PORTAL - ALL INVESTIGATIVE MEASURES WILL BE TAKEN TO ENSURE THAT FALSE ACCUSATIONS ARE NOT MADE. As we are a new group, we will be slowly organising our page to become fully active in a few weeks time.
